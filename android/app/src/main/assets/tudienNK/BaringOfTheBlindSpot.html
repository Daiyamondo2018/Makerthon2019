Baring of the blind spot

* Định nghĩa:

+ Hiện tượng làm cô độc điểm mù.

+ A visual field defect in which there is such a marked contraction of the peripheral temporal visual field that it lies on, or nasal to, the blind spot. Although it may occur in open-angle glaucoma, it is not indicative of the disease as it occurs in the other conditions

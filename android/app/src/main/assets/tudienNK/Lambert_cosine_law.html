+ Xem Diffusion:

+ Scattering of light passing through a heterogeneous medium, or being reflected irregularly by a surface such as sand blasted opal glass surface. Diffusion by a perfectly diffusing surface occurs in accordance with Lambert's cosine law. In this case, the luminance will be the same, ragardless of the viewing direction

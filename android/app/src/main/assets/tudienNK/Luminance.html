Luminance

* Định nghĩa:

+ Độ sáng

+ Ký hiệu: L.

+ Đơn vị: candela per square meter cd/m2 (SI unit). Footlambert, lambert

+ Photometric term characterizing the way in which a surface emits or reflects light in a given direction. It is equal to the luminous intensity measured in a given direction divided by the area of this surface projected on a perpendicular to the dirction considerd.
